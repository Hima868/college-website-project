How to Apply

Applying to our college is easy! Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit your high school/college transcripts and required documents.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Schedule an interview (if required).
  5. Wait for your admission decision!

Financial Aid

We understand that financing your education is important. We offer a variety of financial aid options to help you succeed:

Scholarships

Our college offers merit-based and need-based scholarships. Apply for scholarships as part of the admissions process.

Grants

We provide both federal and state grants to eligible students. These funds are offered based on financial need.

Student Loans

Federal and private loan options are available to help finance your education. Repayment plans are flexible and designed for students.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are the admission requirements?

We require high school transcripts, standardized test scores (if applicable), and a completed application form. Additional documents may be requested during the review process.

2. What scholarships are available?

We offer a range of merit-based and need-based scholarships for both incoming freshmen and current students. For more details, visit our scholarships page.

3. How can I apply for financial aid?

You can apply for financial aid by completing the FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid) and submitting any required documents. Our financial aid office is here to help you through the process.

4. When is the application deadline?

The regular admission deadline is March 1st, while early decision applications are due by November 1st. Be sure to check our admissions page for specific dates.
